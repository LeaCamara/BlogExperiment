Portugal

Portugal is under-rated and even more amazing than I imagined. I found surprising, new, and wonderful things in each area I visited: Lisbon, Cascais, Madeira, and yes, even the sellout Algarves.

Lea Camara, 2016

Caye Caulker, Belize

Ever since Jeff first visited Caye Caulker off the coast of Belize, he has kept coming back. Divers come from around the world to dive the Blue Hole, and, for snorkelers, tours along "Shark Alley" on the coral reef are amazing.

Jeff G., 2012

Italy & Costa Rica

Joel went to Europe for the first time in 2015 and did a three-week tour of Italy. He visited: Florence, Pompeii, Cinque-Terre, Rome, and so many other amazing towns. Then he went to Costa Rica in 2016 to experience a completely different side of the world.

John C., 2016

Africa
